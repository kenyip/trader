## Validity and claim limits

- Earnings timestamps are explicit current retrospective Yahoo Finance records, not a historical point-in-time vendor archive. They support post-announcement event alignment only, not pre-event scheduling alpha.
- BMO maps to the same completed RTH session; AMC maps to the next. Ambiguous regular-session timestamps stand aside. Two in-range TSLA timestamps were excluded instead of guessed.
- Selection uses only completed reaction and pre-outcome features; controls are prior, same symbol/sign, outside earnings windows, without replacement, and pair windows do not overlap.
- Adjusted close outcomes are underlying mechanism evidence only. No next-open fill, option price, IV crush, spread friction, management path, costs, or after-cost PnL was tested.
- The fixed current-rank panel has explicit survivorship/listing bias. ARM has only sealed-era matches and does not contribute to train.
- Structural $200 maximum debit is a planning bound, not observed loss evidence. Proxy-only L0 cannot earn L1.
